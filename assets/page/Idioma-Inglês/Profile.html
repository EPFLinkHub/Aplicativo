About

The Fundão Professional School was created in 1992, through a Program Contract signed on July 29th, between the City Council and the Commercial and Industrial Association of Fundão, with the Ministry of Education. In its first year of operation, 1992/93, the School taught a single course, the Commercial Services Technical Course, in which 48 students enrolled, forming 2 classes.
